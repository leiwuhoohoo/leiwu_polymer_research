## About Me

I am an Associate Researcher / Associate Professor at Sichuan University (starting Jan. 2026), selected for the university's 100 Talent Program and the Ministry of Education Overseas Talent Recruitment Program. I received my Ph.D. in Polymer Chemistry from RWTH Aachen University, Germany in July 2016, and conducted postdoctoral research at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), Princeton University, and UNC Chapel Hill.

My research focuses on functional polymer precision synthesis and the development of advanced functional composite materials, spanning from molecular design to device applications in novel polymer materials, sustainable energy systems, and AI-driven science (AI4Science).

## Research Interests

- **Functional Polymer Synthesis & Polymer Engineering:** ROMP, ATRP/RAFT, ROP, bottlebrush polymers, controlled polymerization
- **Functional Materials for Sustainable & Circular Economy:** degradable and recyclable polymer materials
- **AIE Polymers & Nanocomposites:** aggregation-induced emission polymers, stimulus-responsive smart materials
- **Multi-scale Polymer Simulation & AI4Science:** computational modeling of polymer systems
- **Photoelectrochemistry & Solar Fuel Cells:** electrocatalytic and photoelectrochemical systems
