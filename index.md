## About Me

I am a Research Fellow at Sichuan University (starting Jan. 2026), selected for the university's talent program. I received my Ph.D. in Polymer Chemistry from RWTH Aachen University, Germany in July 2016. I have over 10 years of international research experience across leading institutions, including Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), Princeton University, and UNC Chapel Hill.

My research focuses on functional polymer precision synthesis and the development of advanced functional composite materials, spanning from molecular design to device applications.

## Research Interests

- **Functional Polymer Synthesis & Polymer Engineering:** ROMP, ATRP/RAFT, ROP, bottlebrush polymers, controlled polymerization
- **Functional Materials for Sustainable & Circular Economy:** degradable and recyclable polymer materials
- **AIE Polymers & Nanocomposites:** aggregation-induced emission polymers, stimulus-responsive smart materials
- **Multi-scale Polymer Simulation & AI4Science:** computational modeling of polymer systems
- **Photoelectrochemistry & Solar Fuel Cells:** electrocatalytic and photoelectrochemical systems
